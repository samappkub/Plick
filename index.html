Every year college students embark on the arduous journey of applying to internships. This laborous task quite literally involves typing the same thing over and over again into each company's individual application. A common application already exists for applying to college. Why shouldn't applying for internships be just as simple? That is why we created Plick. Plick allows students to more easily apply to internships by combining all of their apps into one click. At Plick, students fill out an online resume, choose what jobs they want to apply for, then hit submit, and then they are done. As easy and simple as that, just as it should be.
What's Happening?...
With Plick you can apply to Facebook in a flash. All you have you to is enter in a little bit about yourself and off you go.
Do you have what it takes to work at Google? You can find out when you apply through Plick in a one step process. Just fill out the application once, search for internships and Google, and click apply!
SpaceX is always looking for young, inergetic college students and with their internship program in fulll swing you can use Plick to easily apply for one of the positions next summer.
